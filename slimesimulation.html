         After finding inspiration from Sebastian Lague's Youtube video on slime simulation in Unity, I decided to try to make one of my own in ShaderToy. This was truly my first shader project, and I learned a lot throughout the process. The video above displays the shader using Voronoi particle tracking to simulate thousands of different "agents" which have a position and angle defined by a hash function, and they each leave a "pheromone" trail. This, along with a few other rules, combines to create a mesmerizing display of swirls and fluidity.
