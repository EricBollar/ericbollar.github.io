         This is a week-long project that I created back in 2016. The pokedex idea itself stemmed from my discovery of the table functionality in Swift. The actual pokedex was pretty easy and consisted mostly of just filling in each of the 152 first generation pokemon's information. It was actually the search bar that was my most difficult task, and I had a lot of fun figuring out how to finally get it to work. The app was really enjoyable to make.
